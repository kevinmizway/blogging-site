Fun facts about Corgis

The corgi is the smallest herding breed dog.

This dog breed has a long royal history! Queen Elizabeth II has owned more than 30 Corgis since she was a young child.

In Welsh, "cor" means dwarf and "gi" means dog.

Measurements and Diet

A Corgi should weight between 25-30 pounds.

How much you should feed your Corgi depends on how they look each day. If you notice them a bit too chubby, feed them less!

Apples or carrot slices are good options if your dog needs to lose weight.

Obesity concern is extremely important with Corgis!
